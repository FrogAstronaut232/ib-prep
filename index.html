The Curriculum

26 weeks. 8 phases.
Complete preparation.

From your personal story to paper LBOs — a structured path that mirrors how top candidates actually prepare.

WEEKS 1 – 8

Behavioral & Fit

Your story, why banking, strengths & weaknesses, leadership, industry knowledge, culture fit.

WEEKS 9 – 11

Accounting

Income statement, balance sheet, cash flow, three-statement linkages, advanced topics.

WEEKS 12 – 15

Valuation

EV vs equity value, comps, precedent transactions, DCF overview, advanced valuation.

WEEKS 16 – 18

DCF Analysis

Free cash flow, WACC, terminal value, sensitivity analysis, mid-year convention.

WEEKS 19 – 20

Mergers & Acquisitions

M&A fundamentals, accretion/dilution, synergies, sources & uses, pro forma modeling.

WEEKS 21 – 22

Leveraged Buyouts

PE mechanics, debt tranches, return drivers, paper LBO, MOIC, dividend recaps.

WEEKS 23 – 25

Restructuring

Chapter 11 vs 7, absolute priority, fulcrum security, distressed M&A, DIP financing.

WEEK 26

Final Prep

Deal discussions, brain teasers, market sizing. Tie it all together for interview day.
